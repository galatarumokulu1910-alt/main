Architectural Heritage Meets Modern Functionality

Located in the heart of Karaköy, the Galata Greek School offers a unique synthesis of history and contemporary infrastructure. Our spaces are designed to host high-profile fashion shows, art exhibitions, corporate launches, and intimate gala dinners.

Equipped with state-of-the-art AV capabilities, high-speed fiber internet, and climate-controlled galleries, we provide the technical foundation for complex productions within a 19th-century masterpiece.
