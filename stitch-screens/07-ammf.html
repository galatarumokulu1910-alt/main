The Athanasios and Marina Martinou Foundation

"Preserving the legacy of neoclassical Istanbul through the restoration of intellectual heritage."

The Athanasios and Marina Martinou Foundation (AMMF) has played a pivotal role in the comprehensive restoration and visionary transformation of this neoclassical landmark. Their commitment ensures that the Galata Greek School remains a vibrant cultural venue for future generations.

Through meticulous architectural preservation, the foundation has bridged the 120-year history of the school with the demands of the modern era, creating a sanctuary for arts, archives, and academic dialogue in the heart of the city.

Institutional Partnership

The collaboration between the Galata Greek School and AMMF serves as a model for private-public heritage management. By focusing on both the tangible (the building) and the intangible (the education history), we ensure the school continues to contribute to the intellectual fabric of Istanbul.
